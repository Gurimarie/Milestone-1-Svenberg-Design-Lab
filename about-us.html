About Us

Background

The “primus motor” behind Svenberg Design Lab is Guri Marie Svenberg. She started early, as a child, making clothes for her barbies and for herself, whenever she could not find what she wanted, or did not have the money to buy what she wanted.

Through the years she expanded the repertoire to include not only clothing, but also making or re-furbishing furniture, fixing up apartments and making art. Always seeing “what could be” or the ultimate version, and trying to bring it into existence. And with a Bachelor of Arts in Fashion Design from Nuova Accademia di Belle Arti in Milan, Italy, the natural instincts and aesthetic aptitude were honed and developed.

Like a sculpturer seeing a figure within a large, marbled stone, working to “free” the sculpture from the mass surrounding it, we see the potential in the people and things we come across, and our goal is that everything we handle should be closer to their ultimate potential after our encounter.
